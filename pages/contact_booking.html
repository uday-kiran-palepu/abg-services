Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about booking and our services

For emergency situations, we can typically arrange a technician within 30-90 minutes depending on your location and the type of test required. Our emergency hotline (+91 98765 43210) is staffed 24/7 with medical coordinators who can immediately dispatch our nearest available technician.

We need: patient's full name, age, contact number, complete address with landmarks, type of test required, urgency level, and any special medical conditions or mobility limitations. For emergency bookings, we can collect additional details after dispatching the technician.

We currently serve most major areas of Hyderabad including Banjara Hills, Jubilee Hills, Hitech City, Gachibowli, Kondapur, Madhapur, Begumpet, Secunderabad, and Kukatpally. Response times vary from 30-90 minutes depending on the area. Contact us to confirm service availability in your specific location.

We accept cash, UPI payments, credit/debit cards, and online bank transfers. Payment can be made before or after the test completion. For corporate clients, we also offer invoice-based billing with 30-day payment terms.

Yes, you can reschedule or cancel appointments up to 2 hours before the scheduled time without any charges. For cancellations within 2 hours or no-shows, a minimal service charge may apply. Emergency appointments have different cancellation policies due to immediate resource allocation.

Results are delivered via your preferred method: WhatsApp (most common), email, or physical copy delivery. Digital results include secure PDF reports with QR code verification. For complex results, our medical team can schedule a consultation call to explain the findings.
